Block Board

Triveni is one of the leading Manufacturer of Block Board in India. We manufacture a Variety of block board. That consists of “Poplar filler” & “New Zealand Pine” frame. which is persistently filled with Poplar timber with “Zero-Gapping”.

These Board sheets are made by square structure timber with focused filling of softwood which is sandwiched between Poplar Timber Core layers. These layers are all around sewed with Glue on high weight.

These Boards come in thicknesses:-
  • 19MM Single core
  • 22MM Double core
  • 25MM Triple core
